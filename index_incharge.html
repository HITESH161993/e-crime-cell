Welcome to the Crime Management Portol

This website is basically a prototype showing how the police station can move from manual to online.

As an Admin you can register,update and delete a police station and station incharge.

As an incharge you can register an FIR, declare mostwanted criminals as well as detials of missing person.

As a casual user you can search about criminals and you can also able to put your complian in Compliant Window .
